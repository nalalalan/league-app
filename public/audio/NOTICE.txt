Champion stingers are derived from the University of Iowa Musical Instrument Samples.

Source: https://theremin.music.uiowa.edu/MIS.html
The source page states that the samples may be downloaded and used for any projects, without restrictions.

Rendered stingers in this folder combine short excerpts from recorded cello, horn, trumpet, violin, flute,
tam-tam/gong, cymbal roll, crash cymbal, triangle, woodblock, and granite-block samples with synthetic
low pulses and light filtered noise. The current pass uses chord-bounded orchestral swells with a shared
hall tail so the layers blend as one cue. No Riot audio is used.
