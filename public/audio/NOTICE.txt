Champion stingers are derived from the University of Iowa Musical Instrument Samples.

Source: https://theremin.music.uiowa.edu/MIS.html
The source page states that the samples may be downloaded and used for any projects, without restrictions.

Rendered stingers in this folder combine short excerpts from recorded tam-tam/gong, cymbal roll, crash
cymbal, triangle, woodblock, and granite-block samples with synthetic low pulses and filtered noise.
The current pass avoids melodic phrases and uses impact-first sound design: swell, texture, hit, tail.
No Riot audio is used.
