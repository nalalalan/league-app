Champion stingers are derived from the University of Iowa Musical Instrument Samples.

Source: https://theremin.music.uiowa.edu/MIS.html
The source page states that the samples may be downloaded and used for any projects, without restrictions.

Rendered stingers in this folder combine short excerpts from recorded violin, cello, horn, trumpet, flute,
soprano saxophone, tam-tam/gong, cymbal roll, crash cymbal, triangle, woodblock, and granite-block samples
with a low synthetic timpani-style pulse. Each champion file is arranged as a short motif with a rise,
impact, and tail. No Riot audio is used.
