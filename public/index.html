champions

Fit and first rule.

Samira

commit

Reward: dash, reset, R payoff.

Trap: E as panic button.

Rule: Q first, E later.

Caitlyn

range

Reward: sniper space.

Trap: farming forever.

Rule: unavailable until it is time to shoot.

Fizz

reference

Reward: mark, stab, jump, leave.

Trap: expecting every champion to exit like Fizz.

Rule: feel the button shape, then translate it.

Kai'Sa

maybe

Reward: K-pop assassin idea.

Trap: bad walk feel breaks reps.

Rule: visual feel decides spamability.

Miss Fortune

easy

Reward: clean lane damage.

Trap: not enough escape or chaos.

Rule: use when simple farming is the rep.

Ezreal

spam

Reward: QQQ rhythm.

Trap: wrong fantasy.

Rule: keep the spam lesson, not the obligation.

Jhin

style

Reward: clean presentation.

Trap: style without the desired body feel.

Rule: useful to compare, not force.

Ashe

control

Reward: clear range and slows.

Trap: ult pressure feels unrewarding.

Rule: only if calm utility is the goal.

Rammus

no

Reward: simple body.

Trap: physically boring.

Rule: do not turn practice into punishment.
